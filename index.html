About Cleveen

Cleveen Dominguez (pronounced Kle-vin) was given a name that roughly translates to “One of the greatest(in the Navy)” Unfortunately, his parents did not count on him watching Hot Rod and Shaun of The Dead as a kid. So they’ll just have to settle for it meaning a pretty damn good Video Editor. Cleveen currently resides in Los Angeles as a working Video Editor and Assistant Editor. When Cleveen is not working, he’s either writing scripts, playing guitar, or getting bullied by children a lot better than him at video games.
